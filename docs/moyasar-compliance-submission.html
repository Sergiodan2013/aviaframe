2. Business Model & Transaction Types

Consolidator Aviatech Limited operates a white-label flight booking SaaS platform. One Moyasar merchant account will process two distinct transaction types:

Transaction Payer Amount Frequency
Flight ticket purchase End customer (passenger) Variable SAR — airline fare + agency markup + acquiring fee Per booking
Platform subscription Travel agency SAR 300 / month Monthly recurring

Travel agencies embed the AviaFrame widget on their branded domains. End customers pay Consolidator Aviatech Limited directly. Agency service fees are remitted via monthly invoice.

3. Acquiring Fee Disclosure
Payment Method Fee Charged to Customer Disclosed At
Mada 1% of transaction total Checkout — before payment confirmation
Visa / Mastercard 2.75% of transaction total Checkout — before payment confirmation
Bank Transfer / Cash 0% —
The flight search results display: fare + agency markup only. The acquiring fee is added and shown explicitly when the customer selects a payment method at checkout — prior to any payment confirmation.
4. End-to-End Booking Flow

Customer enters route, date, and passenger count in the search widget on the agency's website.

Platform returns available flights. Price shown = airline fare + agency markup (no acquiring fee at this stage).

Customer selects a flight and fills in passenger details (name, passport, contact).

Checkout: customer selects payment method. Price updates in real time to include the acquiring fee for the selected method. Customer sees final payable amount before confirming.

Customer confirms payment. Booking is issued with the airline via DRCT GDS.

E-ticket and itinerary PDF are sent to the customer's email automatically.
