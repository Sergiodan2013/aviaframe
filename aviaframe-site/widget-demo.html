Search & Book Flights

This is the actual AviaFrame booking widget — same one your clients will use.

Use test card 4111 1111 1111 1111 · exp any · CVV any

How it works: Search for a flight → select an offer → fill in passenger details → pay with Moyasar test card. After successful payment, a PDF e-ticket is generated and sent to the email you enter. The order appears in admin.aviaframe.com with status Issued.
