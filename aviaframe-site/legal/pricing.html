Flight Ticket Pricing Breakdown

The total price shown to passengers before payment consists of:

Component Description Amount
Airline fare Base ticket price plus all airline-imposed taxes and surcharges. Set by the airline. Subject to availability until booking is confirmed. Variable — shown in search results
Agency service fee Optional fee configured by the travel agency operating this booking channel. May be a fixed SAR amount or a percentage. Some agencies charge zero. 0 – varies per agency
Payment processing fee Covers card payment processing. Shown transparently at checkout based on selected payment method. Depends on payment method (see below)

Payment Processing Fees by Method

Payment Method Processing Fee Example on SAR 1,000 ticket

Processing fee is calculated on the subtotal (airline fare + agency service fee) and shown on the payment screen before you confirm.

Currency and VAT

All prices are displayed and charged in Saudi Riyal (SAR). VAT (15%) is applied where required by Saudi tax law (ZATCA) and is included in the displayed price.

Price Validity

  • Airline fares are retrieved in real time and may change before final confirmation due to seat availability or provider updates.
  • The price is locked at the payment confirmation step — no additional charges are added after you pay.

Platform Subscription Billing (for Agencies)

The SAR 300/month platform subscription is billed monthly to the registered travel agency. The first charge is due at the start of the active subscription period. Subscription can be cancelled with 30 days written notice to billing@aviaframe.com.

Changes and Refunds

Ticket changes, cancellations, and refunds are subject to the airline's fare rules. See our Refund and Cancellation Policy for full details.
